Stir-fried chicken with lemongrass and chili

Introdution: stir-fried chicken with lemongrass and chili is a flavorful and popular Vietnamese dish. Here's a breakdown of how to make it, combining information from various sources.

Key Ingredients

  • Chicken
    • It's best to use chicken thighs or drumsticks for a more tender result.
  • Lemongrass
    • This is a crucial ingredient, providing the dish's signature aromatic flavor.
  • Chilli
    • Adjust the amount of chili to your desired level of spiciness.
  • Garlic and Shallots
    • These form the aromatic base of the stir-fry.
  • Fish sauces
    • A staple in Vietnamese cuisine, adding savory umami.
  • Other Seasonings
    • Sugar, salt, and other seasonings to balance the flavors.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preperation
    • Clean and cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
    • Prepare the lemongrass by removing the tough outer layers and finely chopping or mincing it.
    • Finely chop the chili, garlic, and shallots.
  2. Marinate the Chicken
    • Marinate the chicken with fish sauce, sugar, and other seasonings. Some recipes add a little bit of turmeric powder for color.
    • Let the chicken marinate for at least 15-30 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  3. Stir-frying
    • Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and shallots, stir-frying until fragrant.
    • Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until it is cooked through.
    • Add the chopped lemongrass and chili, continuing to stir-fry for a few more minutes.
    • Adjust the seasoning with more fish sauce, sugar, or salt as needed.
  4. Serving
    • Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
    • Garnish with fresh herbs like cilantro or mint if desired.

So easy, right? That's how you make the deli stir-fried chicken with lemongrass and chili!
